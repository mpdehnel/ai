We're a small group of professional geeks who get a kick out of making people's lives easier. __Martin__ is a computer scientist and mathematician with a particular interest in community and educational projects, whether software or electronics based. __James__ has been writing educational software for 2-3 years now for schools across the country, after seeing a gap in the market for software that makes a real difference to pupils' and teachers' lives. __Pete__ likes ale, whisky, and Hip-Hopera. When not in his spare time, he's an electronic engineer, specialising in Neural Networks. __Tom__ is also a mathematician and computer scientist. ...and __James__ (yes, another one) likes [penguins](https://www.archlinux.org/ "penguins").
